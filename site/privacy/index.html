Privacy & Terms

Last updated: 2026-07-20

The short version

Your financial records belong to you. We store the structured data you log (amounts, categories, merchants, dates) so we can compute your summaries, and nothing else. No ads, no selling data, no third-party analytics anywhere near your financial data. This website uses Google Analytics to count visits (details below); the product itself does not. You can export everything as CSV or delete your account, with all its data, at any moment, directly from a conversation.

What we store

  • Account: your email address and a password hash (argon2id). Used only for sign-in.
  • Financial records you log: transactions (amount, currency, category, merchant, note, date, optional receipt line items), accounts, budgets and settings (base currency, timezone).
  • Exchange rates: daily reference rates (ECB and national banks) used to convert into your base currency.
  • Technical events: anonymous usage events (tool name, timestamp, error codes) kept for 90 days to keep the service reliable. These events never contain amounts, merchants or notes.

What we never store

  • Receipt photos. Your AI client parses images on its side; only structured numbers reach our server. There is no image storage at all.
  • Bank credentials. Nothing connects to your bank. You log by text, photos or statement exports.
  • Amounts in logs. Server logs and telemetry contain event types and record ids only, never money values, merchants or notes.
  • Your conversations. What you say to Claude or ChatGPT stays between you and your AI provider; we only receive the structured tool calls the AI makes.

Where your data lives

All data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted in the European Union, with row-level security keyed to your account: the database itself enforces that only your session can read your rows. Access tokens are issued via OAuth 2.0 with PKCE and can be revoked at any time by disconnecting the server from your AI client.

Website analytics

These marketing pages use Google Analytics to understand how many people visit and which pages they read (page views, referrer, approximate region). It runs only on this website: the MCP endpoint and your financial data never touch it, and it cannot see who you are inside the product. If you block it with a content blocker, everything here keeps working.

Your rights (GDPR)

  • Portability: say "export my transactions" and you get a full CSV, anytime.
  • Erasure: say "delete my account" and your account with every record is removed. This is a built-in tool, not a support ticket.
  • Access and correction: every record can be listed, searched and edited through the same conversation.
